## Goal

Develop a thorough understanding of what you want to build or accomplish. Ask clarifying questions to explore the problem space until you are confident and ready to proceed with implementation.

## Steps

1. **Acknowledge the request** - Briefly confirm you understand the high-level goal based on the user's initial description.

2. **Explore the "why"** - Ask why the user wants to do this. Understanding the underlying motivation helps ensure the solution addresses the real problem.

3. **Clarify scope** - Determine what is included and what is explicitly excluded from this effort.

4. **Identify constraints** - Ask about any technical constraints, deadlines, dependencies, or preconditions that should guide the approach.

5. **Understand the user** - Consider their experience level and preferences when deciding how much detail vs. abstraction to provide.

6. **Probe for edge cases** - Ask about known edge cases, error scenarios, or special conditions that should be handled.

7. **Wait for the signal** - The user will explicitly indicate when they are satisfied and ready to proceed.

8. **Switch to code mode** - When the user signals readiness, use `switch_mode` to transition to "code" mode with a clear summary of the plan.
